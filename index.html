Most of my entries this year have come from the Undercut & rally or (U&R) setup off of key EMAs and weekly pivots

With the market continuing to consolidate and some of the best stocks from the last few months building big bases

Its an incredibly important concept to understand:

For example I caught a beautiful move on $MRNA last week

Over +30% on shares in just 1 week...

This setup and entry came right off of a key moving average after the stock undercut the lows of its flag and reclaimed

Failed Breakdown = Inflection Point.

When a stock undercuts a key support level like a moving average or lows of a base

-Sellers get trapped shorting the hole
-Early buyers stop out of their positions

& when the stocks ends up reclaiming that level and putting in a "failed breakdown"... shorts will have to cover quickly adding to the buying pressure

Early buyers will be forced to add back to the position quickly adding to the buying pressure

This creates a strong influx of demand & now you have a shift in control, turning a previous low into a "high probability" entry point.

The key of this setup is the "reclaim" as that sets up the defined risk for the setup

When a low is put in and the stock reclaims the key support level it broke, you can now use low of day as your stop with tight risk

My process for UnR's looks like this:

1.) Find a tight base in a strong stock into a key support level (50 DEMA)
2.) Set alerts for an undercut
3) Wait for the break and reclaim
4) Enter on the reclaim
5) Set stop loss at low of day

You now get an incredibly low risk high probability entry into a leading name you've been stalking

The market environment this year has been rewarding buyers who enter at these lows, while chasing breakouts has been brutal.

U&R setups give you defined risk, a clear structure, and the ability to stay ahead of momentum before it fully expresses itself.

The ultimate asymmetric setup.

防守型和周期型板块也在同步接棒，工业板块带着道指创了历史新高， 医疗保健、必需消费品、住宅建筑商、区域银行、生物科技这些此前被冷落的方向都在吸引资金。

存储和芯片板块的操作节奏

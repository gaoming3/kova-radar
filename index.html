BREAKING: $META is up 8.6% in premarket after Bloomberg reported the company is building a cloud business to sell excess AI computing power.

Meta is developing two separate products: one that sells access to its AI models hosted on its own infrastructure, similar to AWS Bedrock, and one that sells raw computing capacity, similar to CoreWeave.

The initiative is called Meta Compute, led by Meta's head of infrastructure, a leader from its Superintelligence Labs, and Meta President Dina Powell McCormick.

Zuckerberg said in May: "Almost every week there are different companies that come to us asking if we have compute that they could buy from us at some premium to what we've bought it at."

Meta has committed hundreds of billions to data centers and AI chips.

A cloud business turns that cost center into a direct revenue stream, the same model that built AWS, Azure, and Google Cloud into businesses generating tens of billions per quarter.

3️⃣ 有现成的财务健康评分，帮你快速判断这公司破产、暴雷的概率。财报能看近 5 年的数据。

怎么用：

先在 Finviz 设好条件(板块、市值、估值、盈利增长这些)，筛出一批股票，再在 Stock Analysis 搜索，每只重点看五样:

· 营收和利润是不是每年都在增长
· 增长靠的是哪块业务
· 分析师怎么看
· 有没有暴雷风险
· 估值如何
